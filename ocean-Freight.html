    Ocean Freight

    Less than Container Load (LCL)

    No shipment is too small for us, nor destination too far away. With Scan-Shipping's access to the most comprehensive global network of guaranteed direct consolidated ocean LCL services, we can assure the least amount of physical handling for your merchandise to reduce the  risk of damages, shortages as well as market leading transit time from origin to destination all serviced on premium shipping lines at competitive pricing.


    See our Sailing Schedule.

    Full Container Load (FCL)

    Anyone can offer a FCL price and transit time from point-to-point. Scan-Shipping offers multiple carrier options with differentiated and multi-tiered service levels catering to all types of cargo, being of high-value with a need for speed or low-value with a greater emphasis on cost. Our deep and long-lasting carrier relationships combined with our superior buying power, means it is rare we cannot create a solution meeting the requirements of even the most discerning client.

    Container Specifications tool.

    Rail Transport

    When you need your shipment faster than ocean and cheaper than air, consider our weekly rail service from China to Europe.

    Offering rail services with pre-carriage to Wuhan for consolidation and deconsolidation in Warsaw for on-carriage to final destination; we can provide a total estimated transit time of approximately 18-20 days.

    Try our service and find the best solution for your business with fixed rates from a variety of China gateway cities, competitive ex works rates and door delivery rates in Northern Europe.


    For more information, rail@scan-shipping.com.
